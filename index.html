The Kormoon Journey

When we founded Kormoon, we had a clear vision: to revolutionise how businesses navigate the complex landscape of data privacy and protection. Understanding both the legal and technical challenges companies face, we recognised that organisations needed a better way to manage their regulatory compliance obligations.

By combining artificial intelligence with expert legal guidance, we created a solution that made compliance more accessible and manageable. Our innovative approach resonated strongly with the industry, leading to a significant milestone: our acquisition by Privitar. This achievement validated not only our mission but proved that our experimental approach to transforming data privacy compliance could succeed.

But the story didn't end there‚Äîit evolved.

At Privitar, Kormoon became the Data Compliance Navigator (DCN), helping organisations determine exactly what data protections were required. While Privitar focused on protecting sensitive data, DCN answered the crucial first question: what needs protecting, and why?

Within Informatica, these capabilities expanded further. We brought data access management and protection into a comprehensive data governance platform‚Äîensuring organisations could not only identify compliance requirements but enforce them at scale across their entire data estate.

Now, as part of Salesforce, the vision has reached new heights. The governance foundations we built are helping unlock the potential of Agentforce, ensuring that AI agents can operate with robust data governance guardrails. From helping startups navigate GDPR to enabling enterprise AI‚Äîwhat began as Kormoon continues to shape how businesses responsibly use their data.

Paul McCormack, Founder of Kormoon

Who is Kormoon for?

Corporations leveraging data

  • A cost-effective way to access typically expensive information.
  • A faster and safer way to obtain information about how to comply with data compliance requirements.
  • A means of identifying and mitigating risks.
  • Providing greater internal efficiency by reducing unnecessary human intervention, saving time and money.

Users looking to remove barriers

  • A means of identifying and understanding data compliance risks earlier on in projects.
  • Provides the user with the right rule and data compliance steps across varying contexts to ensure consistency.
  • A fast and reliable way to engage with the key team members when necessary.
  • Understandable and actionable advice.

Legal and compliance teams

  • Improves productivity by automating and removing routine internal data requests.
  • A more sustainable way to gather and retain data compliance rules (especially across jurisdictions).
  • Ensuring precise and consistent advice.
  • Improved engagement with internal clients and stakeholders.
